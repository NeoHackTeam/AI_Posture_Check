Precision Engineering for Your Health

Posture Pal isn't just a timer. It's a sophisticated ergonomic analysis tool built with customizable parameters.

Neck Angle Analysis

Real-time calculation of your cervical spine angle. Alerts trigger when you exceed your safe threshold (e.g., >15°).

Shoulder Threshold

Detects slouching by monitoring shoulder level variance. Keeps your upper back aligned and engaged.

Distance Monitoring

Ensures you maintain a healthy screen distance to reduce eye strain and forward head posture.

Smart Break Intervals

Customizable Pomodoro-style timers integrated with posture fatigue detection.

Warning Delay

Intelligent buffer zones prevent false alarms when you just need to reach for your coffee.

Progress Tracking

Visualize your posture improvements over time with detailed session analytics.

Behind The Code

The Mission

As students and developers, we spend hours glued to our screens. We realized that back pain, shoulder pain, and eye strain was affecting our focus and long-term health.

Posture Pal was born out of a desire to combine computer vision AI with ergonomic science. We didn't just want to build another timer app; we wanted to build a smart assistant that actually sees and understands how you work.
